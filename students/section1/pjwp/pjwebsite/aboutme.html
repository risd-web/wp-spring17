My name is Poornima Jhanji and I was born in New Delhi, India. I moved to Jaipur, the city of history, amazing food, and wonderful stories about kings and queens. When I was little, my dad was offered a job in America and had my mother and I move to Bay Ridge, in New York. I lived there for about 5 years, and later moved to Edison, New Jersey.
