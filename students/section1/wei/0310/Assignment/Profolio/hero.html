流浪的英雄
Heros
on the Way

Size: A0


In memory of the victims of White Terror in Taiwan.
In 1949, the R.O.C. government lost the Chinese Civil War and retreated to last territory—Taiwan. A censorship followed executed and had endured for forty-two years. During this period, called "White Terror", People's rights of seeing, talking, listening, even living are deprived. In this episode of history against Communism, countless innocent people in Taiwan died without a reason.
