Julie Cho's cyber room

I designed my own portfolio website, introducing who I am as a graphic designer. Many people use website templates for presenting their portfolios, but I personally think the design of the website also can stand for one of the biggest parts of my design principle. The concept of my portfolio website is a ‘my personal room’. Screen-based works are mostly two dimensional, and sometimes it is very hard to bring spatial quality. For my portfolio website, I experiment with the idea of ‘space’ and explore more about how to bring the three dimensional aspect into web design. For my website, I put some materials that occupy my room, such as a green carpet, flower printed beddings, a wooden long board, etc. This is a cyber version of my room which shows my personality and my lifestyle. Also, you can check my portfolios hanging on the wall.





What I learned

During this spring semester, I learned how to manipulate digital space freely and professionally. Including learning technical skills and coding languages, I also learned how to build interactive website that user can experience.
