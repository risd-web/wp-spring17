Direction

  1. Put pork belly in a bowl. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil and fish sauce.
  2. Toss well to coat and let marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. Set a heavy-bottomed soup pot over medium heat.
  4. Melt butter, then add pork belly mixture and let it cook gently for 5 minutes.
  5. Add onion and cook, stirring, until softened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Turn heat to medium high and add kimchi, gochujang and gochugaru.
  7. Add kimchi juice and water and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat to a brisk simmer and cook for 20 minutes.
  9. Add tofu and stir gently to combine.
  10. Garnish with scallions.
