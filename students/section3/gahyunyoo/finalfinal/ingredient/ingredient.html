Ingredients

  1. Pound kimchi, cut into bite size pieces
  2. ¼ cup kimchi brine
  3. ½ pound pork shoulder (or pork belly)
  4. ½ package of tofu
  5. Green onions
  6. Medium onion, sliced (1 cup)
  7. 1 teaspoon salt
  8. 1 teaspoons sugar
  9. 2 teaspoons Korean hot pepper flakes
  10. 1 tablespoon hot pepper paste
  11. Teaspoon sesame oil
  12. Cups of anchovy stock
