Show me how to bake Salted Caramel Mudslide Cupcakes!

Ingredients(Sponge)

  • 350g plain flour
  • 60g cocoa powder
  • 370g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • pinch of salt
  • 150ml chocolate chips
  • 3 large eggs
  • 240ml cooled coffee
  • 240ml/g buttermilk
  • 210ml vegetable oil
  • Half batch of Salted Caramel

Ingredients(Icing)

  • 300g unsalted butter (soft)
  • 540g icing sugar (sifted)
  • the other half of the Salted Caramel (150ml)
