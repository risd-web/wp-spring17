Ingredients(Sponge)

  • 235g Self raising flour
  • 15g Cocoa powder

  • 4 Large free-range eggs

  • 1.5 tsp Cider vinegar

Ingredients(Icing)

  • 150g Soft unsalted butter
  • 240g Cream Cheese
  • 840g Icing sugar
