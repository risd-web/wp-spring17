There are 4 sections to the questionnaire, each containing rows of traits. In each row, choose the trait you identify more with. The column with more highlighted traits determines your letter. For example, if you have 4 traits highlighted in the Extraverts column and 3 traits highlighted in the Introverts column, you are considered Extraverted (E). Finish the four sections to determine your 4-letter type.

WHAT ENERGIZES YOU?

EXTRAVERTS = E

are generally sociable

are focused on the outer world

get energy by spending time with others

talk a lot and start conversations

speak first, then think

are quick to take action

have many friends and many interests

INTROVERTS = I

are generally quiet

are focused on their inner world

get energy by spending time alone

mostly listen and wait for others to talk first

think first, then speak

are slow to take action

have a few deep friendships and refined interests
